Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

The Best Western Plus Meridian Hotel values your trust and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or stay at our property in Nairobi, Kenya.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when making a reservation, requesting a proposal, or communicating with our front desk. This may include:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and transaction history (processed securely via our payment gateways).
  • Stay Preferences: Room types, dietary requirements, and specific requests made during the booking process.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect primarily to fulfill our hospitality services to you. This includes processing reservations, managing payments, sending booking confirmations, and personalizing your stay. We may also use your email to send post-stay feedback requests or promotional offers, which you can opt out of at any time.

3. Data Security & Third Parties

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access. We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary data with trusted third-party service providers (such as the Best Western global reservation network or secure payment processors) strictly for the purpose of facilitating your stay.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and optimize our digital presence. You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies, though this may affect certain functionalities of our online booking engine.

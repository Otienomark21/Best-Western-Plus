Essential Guest Services

We anticipate your needs. Beyond wellness, the Meridian provides comprehensive support services to ensure your stay in Nairobi is seamless, productive, and secure.

Airport Transfers

Secure, chauffeured pick-up and drop-off services to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) available upon request.

Secure Parking

Complimentary, 24-hour guarded basement parking exclusively for our resident guests and conferencing delegates.

Business Center

Fully equipped workstations with printing, scanning, and high-speed internet access for your last-minute corporate needs.

24Hr Concierge

Our team is ready to arrange excursions to Nairobi National Park, the Giraffe Centre, or the Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage, alongside standard transit bookings.

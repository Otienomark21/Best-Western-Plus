Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to the Best Western Plus Meridian Hotel. By accessing our website or booking a reservation, you agree to be bound by the following terms and conditions.

1. Check-In & Check-Out

Standard check-in time is 12:00 PM (Noon) East African Time. Check-out time is 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Guests must present a valid government-issued photo ID or passport and a valid credit card upon check-in.

2. Reservation & Guarantee Policy

All reservations must be guaranteed with a valid credit card or an approved company Local Purchase Order (LPO). The hotel reserves the right to pre-authorize credit cards prior to arrival. Cash payments are accepted; however, a security deposit may be required at check-in.

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled date of arrival (by 12:00 PM local time). Failure to cancel within this timeframe, or in the event of a no-show, will result in a penalty charge equivalent to one night's room rate plus applicable taxes. Non-refundable and advance purchase rates are subject to their specific promotional terms.

4. Hotel Policies

  • Smoking: We are a 100% smoke-free property indoors. A deep cleaning fee of $250 will be charged to any guest found smoking in their room.
  • Pets: Pets are not permitted, with the exception of certified service animals.
  • Damage: Guests are liable for any damage caused to hotel property during their stay.

5. Liability

While the hotel employs 24-hour security and CCTV surveillance, guests are encouraged to use the electronic safes provided in their rooms. The hotel assumes no liability for lost, stolen, or damaged personal property.

For further inquiries regarding our policies, please contact our reservations desk at reservation@bwplusmeridian.com.
